Article 12

\

1. Devices other than those which are custom made or intended for clinical investigations considered to meet the essential requirements referred to in Article 3 must bear the \ ►M2\ CE marking ◄ of conformity.

\

2. The \ ►M2\ CE marking ◄ of conformity, as shown in Annex 9, must appear in a visible, legible and indelible form on the sterile pack and, where appropriate, on the sales packaging, if any, and on the instruction leaflet.

\

\ ▼M2\

\

It must be followed by the identification number of the notified body responsible for implementation of the procedures set out in Annexes 2, 4 and 5.

\

3. The affixing of markings on the devices which are likely to deceive third parties as to the meaning and form of the CE marking shall be prohibited. Any other marking may be affixed to the packaging or to the instruction leaflet accompanying the device provided that the visibility and legibility of the CE marking is not hereby reduced.
